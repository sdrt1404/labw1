At the beginning of 2018, the city had 4 million inhabitants. There is also a significant Armenian diaspora. In Los Angeles, there are about 50 thousand homeless people.

Los Angeles is famous for its beaches and famous area-Hollywood. This city has many nicknames — it's just LA, and Southland (country in the south), and the City of Angels, and Lotosville, and El Pueblo ("city" in Spanish).
